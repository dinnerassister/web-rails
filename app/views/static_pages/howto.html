What's Dinner Assister?

Meal planning made easy.

  • 1

    Add your recipes

    Whether it's your grandma's secret or a recipe you found online, add it to your online recipe box. Tag it with "main dish" to add it to your meal plan. Add other tags for easy organization. Recipe can be public or private.
  • 2

    Create your meal plans

    Creating a meal plans is as easy as hitting a button. Don't like what you see? Swap what you don't and replace it with your desired recipes. One recipe is not enough? Add as many recipes as you can eat ;)
  • 3

    Get your groceries

    Ready to go to the market? Bring your phone and check off items you got.
  • 4

    Prepare your meal

    No need to wonder what to make today. Viewing today's meal plan is only two click/tap away.
