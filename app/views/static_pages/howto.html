What's Dinner Assister?

A website to help you plan your meals.

  • 1

    Add your recipes

    Whether it's your grandma's secret or a recipe you found online, add it to your online recipe box. You can tags the recipes for easy organization. You can choose to share the recipe with public or keep it private.
  • 2

    Create your meal plans

    Creating a meal plans is as easy as hitting a button. However, if you don't like what we generated, you can swap out meals or add more recipes to a meal.
  • 3

    Get your groceries

    Ready to go to the market? Bring your phone and check off items you got.
  • 4

    Prepare your meal

    No need to wonder what to make today. Viewing today's meal plan is only two click/tap away.
